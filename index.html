Harnessing Advanced Technologies for Targeted Environmental Solutions

We provide a comprehensive range of software solutions for air dispersion modeling, human health and ecological risk assessment, emissions inventory, regulatory compliance, and emergency release scenarios.

Air Dispersion Modeling
Air dispersion modeling utilizes data from emission sources and meteorological conditions to simulate the transport and dispersion of pollutants in the atmosphere, enabling the calculation of pollutant concentrations at specific locations.
Emissions Management
Emissions management entails the calculation, collection, and consolidation of emission data from various sources. This data is essential for developing emissions inventories and supporting air dispersion modeling efforts.
Risk Assessment
Risk assessment extends beyond air dispersion modeling to evaluate the fate and transport of pollutants through processes such as deposition, runoff, and bioaccumulation, providing a comprehensive understanding of their potential impacts on human health and the environment.

Emergency Release
This is a specialized form of air dispersion modeling focused on accidental releases, often involving substances denser than air. It is used to assess potential incident scenarios and develop effective emergency response plans.
Met Data
We offer high-quality modeled meteorological data for any location worldwide, available in AERMET, CALMET, and CALPUFF-compatible formats to support a wide range of air quality modeling applications.
Training
Our comprehensive training programs combine hands-on computer-based instruction with the latest research insights. We offer specialized courses in air dispersion modeling, covering both AERMOD and CALPUFF systems.
