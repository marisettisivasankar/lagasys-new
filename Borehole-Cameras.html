Cameras

GeoVISION™ Borehole Video Systems from Allegheny Instruments are robust and reliable, customizable for applications ranging from 1-inch piezometer tubes to 10-foot diameter mine shafts. Equipped with manual or electric winches and up to 2,000 feet (600 meters) of cable, these systems are ideal for water wells, environmental wells, geotechnical borings, caisson foundations, drilled shafts, and shallow oil & gas wells.



Size Matters

As pioneers in portable downhole video systems, we engineer our cameras to be exceptionally compact and lightweight. Our 3/4-inch (20 mm) Nano camera enables well inspections without pump removal—see demonstration. Its slim profile and beveled clamp make it ideal for tool-fishing operations, reducing snag risks. The Dual Scan Micro (1⅛" diameter) holds the title as the smallest controllable-sideview borehole camera available.

Lenses

We utilize a variety of different lenses to provide optimal video images over a wide range of well diameters. Our lenses are sized to provide the maximum field of view without the distortion effects produced by fish-eye lenses. We can also instll custom lenses to fit your project's requiremants.

Cable

Our lightweight polytheylene-sheathed cable consist of two outer copper-wrapped stell wires for power and ground and an inner copper wire for the video signal. The cable can be easily replaced, so a system initially purchased with 325 feet of cable can be readily upgraded to a 1000 foot system.

Lighting

Proper lighting is critical for optimal video image quality. Our cameras have on-board high intensity LED lights. The Dual Scan Micro has three sets of independently-controlled LED light banks, which can be adjusted on the control panel depending upon downlole conditions and bore diameters. The Nano's LED lights are recessed six inches above the lens, thus providing optimal backlighting conditions. For large diameter wells/boreholes and challenging water calrity situation, we offer multiple auxiliry light setups, including the auxiliry dual setup which attached directly to the camera and two auxiliry 8 light rings which mount above the camera assembly.

Control

Our Deluxe winch system offers joystick-controlled controllable-sideview with either the Dual Scan Camera or the Standard Camera mounted on our Pan Tilt COntroller. With the Dual Scan the joystick is used to toggle between the downview or sideview lenses and to rotate the camera body. The Pan Tilt Controller aloows the operator to pan and tilt the Standard Camera lens in any direction including back up the borehole.
