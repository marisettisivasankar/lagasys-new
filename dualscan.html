Dual Scan Borehole Camera
Advanced Downview & Sideview Borehole Imaging
The GeoVISIONTM Dual-Scan is Allegheny Instruments' most advanced borehole camera, featuring downview and sideview modules, 360° rotation, and high intensity LED lighting for unparalleled video imaging in bores as small as 1.5" (4 cm) and depths up to 2,000 feet (600 meters).

Overview

The Dual Scan features both downview and sideview cameras, allowing operators to toggle between views and use a joystick for a complete 360° scan of the borehole. This dual capability ensures comprehensive inspection and documentation of borehole conditions.

  • Standard NTSC resolution, 4:3 aspect ratio
  • Three banks of high intensity white LEDs, individually controlled
  • Rated for up to 1,000 psi and 2,000 feet (600 meters) depth
  • Compatible exclusively with the Deluxe winch
