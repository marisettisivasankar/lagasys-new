Nano Camera
Ultra-Compact Borehole Imaging
The GeoVISIONTM Nano is Allegheny Instruments' smallest borehole camera, designed for high-resolution imaging in wells as small as 0.75" (19 mm) in diameter. Its robust construction and advanced optics make it ideal for challenging, small-diameter borehole inspections.

Overview

The Nano Camera is engineered for ultra-compact boreholes, providing clear video in the tightest spaces. Its small size, rugged build, and high-intensity LED lighting ensure reliable performance in demanding environments.

  • Fits wells as small as 0.75" (19 mm) in diameter
  • High-resolution video output
  • Rugged, waterproof construction
  • Bright, energy-efficient LED illumination
